As you re-design your life, use these powerful mindsets to get the most out of your quest.
Radical honesty
If you want to design the life that is just right for you, be ready to let go of the old identities, roles, beliefs, patterns and behaviours than no longer serve you. It requires a lot of courage and a radical honesty mindset.
Experimentation
There is a lot of uncertainty in any life. There is even more uncertainty in an extraordinary life. When you are a trailblazer, there often is no one to show you a path, you will have to experiment, learn and correct the path as you go.
Fail fast
You will fail, of course you will. Failure is an inevitable part of any great endeavor. But this is part of the fun. Don't dwell on your mistakes; instead, welcome them, embrace the lessons they offer, and use them as stepping stones toward personal growth.
Dream big
Living an average life is like swimming in a crowded Red Ocean, filled with competition and limitations. Dare to dream big and create your own Blue Ocean of possibilities. By setting audacious goals, you open yourself up to a world of endless opportunities and fulfillment.
Just do it
Let's admit, you've already spent enough time thinking things through, worrying and contemplating potential scenarios. Now it's time to get out of your head and into your life. Avoid the analysis paralysis and start writing, reflecting, designing, trying. In no time you will be living your dream life.
